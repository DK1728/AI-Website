AI Ethics & Challenges

With great power comes great responsibility. Let's explore the ethical implications and risks of artificial intelligence.

Key Ethical Issues

  • Bias in AI: Algorithms can reflect and even amplify societal biases.

  • Job Displacement: Automation threatens traditional employment sectors.

  • Privacy: Facial recognition and data collection raise serious privacy concerns.

  • Accountability: Who is responsible when AI makes a mistake?

  • Surveillance: Ethical issues in government and corporate use of AI for tracking citizens.

Did You Know?

In 2021, the EU proposed strict regulations on AI, classifying some uses—like social scoring—as "unacceptable risk."
