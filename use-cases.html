How AI Is Used in the Real World

AI is transforming industries from healthcare to entertainment. Here are some amazing applications of artificial intelligence:

Healthcare

AI helps doctors diagnose diseases from X-rays, predict patient outcomes, and personalize treatment.

Education

Smart tutors and AI tools personalize learning and help students master complex subjects.

Self-driving Cars

AI powers autonomous vehicles that can navigate traffic and avoid accidents.

Finance

AI algorithms predict stock trends, detect fraud, and automate investment strategies.
